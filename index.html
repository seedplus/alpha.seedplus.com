Finalists announced

29 October, right here

Post application, five start-ups from across the four cities will be shortlisted as finalists and recognised as one of the best early stage start-ups in ASEAN. These start-ups will be assigned two primary mentors for three months, one each from AWS and SeedPlus, and be given access to business and technical experts from AWS and SeedPlus.

All finalists will be on-boarded to AWS and given a USD 25,000 Activate portfolio package. AWS will also provide mentorship on well architected and SA best practices, security and AWS strategic services in AI / ML / KRADL / IOT topics.

The dedicated SeedPlus partner will work with the finalists on issues ranging from strategy and business development to product and recruitment to support the founders in building sustainable businesses. SeedPlus will also consider investing seed funding at the conclusion of the process.
