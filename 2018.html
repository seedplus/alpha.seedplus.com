Project Alpha 2018

2018 saw the launch of Project Alpha in Kuala Lumpur, Jakarta, Bangkok and Singapore. Together with acclaimed entrepreneurs and local heroes, we hosted events in each of these 4 countries to inspire and target the next generation of founders. SeedPlus and AWS picked seven startups from the submissions to receive:

  • Access to business and technical experts from AWS and SeedPlus
  • USD 25,000 Activate portfolio package from AWS
  • Market recognition from AWS and SeedPlus as a high-growth, high-potential ASEAN startup
  • Potential seed round funding from SeedPlus

Finalists announced

29 October 2018

We are proud to unveil the finalists of Project Alpha 2018. Ranging from chatbots to workforce management, we believe they represent the best of the region's early-stage startups:

42Lab

Singapore: 42Lab envisions to develop future leaders in the field of BioScience by providing students an early and better understanding of Biotechnology during their middle to high school years.

DuitinAja

Jakarta: DuitinAja is building a more efficient advertising platform.

Jasaconnect

Jakarta: Jasaconnect builds a platform to manage and transform onsite workforces for the on-demand age, with a focus on maintenance and repair.

Lapasar

Kuala Lumpur: Lapasar revolutionizes corporate procurement by integrating convenience with a diverse marketplace filled with office supply vendors of different shapes and sizes.

Noodle Factory

Singapore: Noodle Factory builds Walter, a personal chatbot mentor that can be used both by individuals and by enterprises, helping with learning, compliance, retention and procedures.

Pipwave

Kuala Lumpur: Pipwave by Dynamic Podium is a simple, reliable, and cost-effective way to accept payments online, with built-in fraud detection.

Quest

Bangkok: Quest provides online real-time platform to allow a pathway to the digital literacy of the 21st century focusing on language expertise in soft skills and coding skills.

All finalists will be on-boarded to AWS and given a USD 25,000 Activate portfolio package. AWS will also provide mentorship on best practices for web architecture, security and advanced AWS service usage for AI/ML, analytics and IoT.

A dedicated SeedPlus partner will work with the finalists on issues ranging from strategy and business development to product and recruitment, and support the shortlisted founders in building sustainable businesses. SeedPlus will also consider investing seed funding at the conclusion of the process.

How they pitched

Participants submits five-page pitch decks. The template highlights all the relevant information Project Alpha is looking for founders are free to add anything that would help in getting to know your team, your company or your market better.

There are also some very objective eligibility criteria:

  • HQ or main operations must be in a Project Alpha city (i.e. Kuala Lumpur, Bangkok, Jakarta, Singapore)
  • Previously raised no more than US$250,000 in start-up funding
  • Submit a 5 page PDF deck in English using the provided pitch deck template.
  • All applications submitted after the closing date will not be reviewed.
